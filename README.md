# Shower Thought
## Introduction
* We've discovered an intriguing website where people share their whimsical musings from the shower, which we find rather amusing. We aim to curate these delightful snippets into a clean interface, showcasing these crystallizations of human ingenuity. We hope you'll enjoy browsing them.

## Philosophy
* In daily life, we're constantly pressured by events and demands to think according to specific procedures and steps, turning thought into an exercise in thinking for its own sake. Showering offers a rare opportunity for complete relaxation. The soothing flow of water creates a natural ‘thinking space’ free from external distractions, allowing the mind to roam freely and generate wildly imaginative ideas. These thoughts deserve to be recorded and preserved, for their emergence is imbued with randomness and creativity, showcasing humanity's greatest capacity for inventiveness.
## Technical Implementation
* The display interface was developed using p5.js.
* The webpage design employs a monochrome palette with minimal ornamentation, emphasising the core contemplative content. This visualisation adopts a de-UIed, minimalist digital art exhibition label style, placing the content itself at the visual centre.

* We identified data directly readable by the webpage's computer language, scraping and extracting its content. Only the title and like count are retained for display, with other elements omitted to maintain conciseness.

* Finally, we employed Gemini3 to organise the code structure and filter the JSON content.
## Usage Guide
* Upon entering the webpage, you will randomly encounter a shower thought. To view additional thoughts, click anywhere on the screen to refresh the page, which will display a new whimsical idea.
